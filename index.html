A Global Listening Tour

KIS was not born in a lab, but in the living rooms of thousands of parents. We have reached out to parental organizations worldwide to understand exactly what is needed to protect the next generation.

What Parents Told Us
  • Hardware Security: Disabling a camera via software isn't enough; parents want it physically removed.
  • The Browser Barrier: Access to the unfiltered web is the primary gateway to harmful content.
  • Compact Design: Rugged phones shouldn't be too heavy for a teenager's hand or an adult's pocket.
The Evidence

The Science of Presence

Our design is driven by clinical research into neurological health and the benefits of a digital detox.

Impact on the Frontal Cortex

Research indicates that constant notifications and algorithmic loops interfere with the development of the pre-frontal cortex—the brain's center for impulse control and focus. By removing these distractions, KIS supports cognitive growth in children and recovery in adults.

Benefits of a Digital Detox
  • Reduced Anxiety: Lower cortisol levels by eliminating the "infinite scroll."
  • Better Rest: Improved melatonin production by removing late-night blue light traps.
  • Deep Focus: Minimalist users report a significant increase in the ability to reach "Flow State" in work and study.
