The Background

The Digital Shift

The era of the "pocket computer" began with a promise of freedom. However, technology shifted from empowering the user to capturing the user. We have moved from a world of tools that help us live, to a world of devices that demand we stay.

The Problem

A Crisis of Attention

Modern smartphones are designed for distraction. **The average adult now spends between 4 and 5 hours every day on their device—equivalent to roughly 40% of their waking life.**

The Solution

Reclaimed Focus

The solution is a return to utility. By choosing technology that respects your time, you can immerse yourself in deep work and accomplish your goals with a sense of peace.
