01. The Background / Problem

Digital Overload

Modern smartphones have become predatory. They are designed to steal our time through infinite scrolls and constant notifications, turning a helpful tool into a source of anxiety.

02. The Solution

Intentional Technology

We believe technology should serve you, then get out of the way. The solution is a return to utility: devices that help you accomplish a task without trying to own your attention.

03. The KIS Phone

A Highly Functional Tool

The KIS Phone provides all the good—essential communication, high-speed tools, and modern reliability—without any of the bad. No tracking, no algorithms, no bloatware. Pure functionality.

04. K.I.S.S. (Keep It Super Simple)

Designed for Kids

Children deserve a safe entry into the digital world. Our "Super Simple" interface provides essential connectivity for kids without the addictive hooks and dangers of the standard modern web.
