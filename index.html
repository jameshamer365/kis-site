The Problem

A Crisis of Attention

Modern smartphones are designed for distraction. The average adult now spends roughly 40% of their waking life staring at a screen. We have moved from a world of tools that help us live, to a world of devices that demand we stay.

The Solution

Reclaimed Focus: The KIS Phone

The solution is a return to utility. By choosing technology that respects your time, you reclaim your productivity and your peace of mind. The KIS Phone is a professional-grade tool designed to get out of your way.

The Research

Evidence-Based Design

Our handsets are driven by feedback from thousands of parents and clinical research into neurological health.

Global Parental Outreach

We have reached out to parental organizations worldwide. The consensus for the KiSS (Junior) model was clear: hardware-level camera removal and the total elimination of web browsers are essential for child safety.

The Frontal Cortex & Cognitive Health

Research suggests that infinite scrolls and constant notifications interfere with the development of the pre-frontal cortex. KIS provides the "digital detox" necessary for cognitive recovery and deep focus.
